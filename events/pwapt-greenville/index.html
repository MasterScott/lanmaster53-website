PWAPT Greenville

Price

$1,500.00

Description

Practical Security Services (PractiSec) is thrilled to be partnering with BSides Greenville to bring world class training to upstate South Carolina! This training opportunity is a 3-day presentation of Practical Web Application Penetration Testing (PWAPT) with Tim "lanmaster53" Tomes. This is not your average PWAPT offering. In addition to world class training, you'll have the opportunity to network and share knowledge with other professionals across the region and industry at one of the region's best conferences. For specific details regarding course materials and prerequisites, see the PWAPT section of the PractiSec training page. IMPORTANT: BSides Greenville event tickets must be purchased separately. Training tickets are not valid for entry into the conference. Event tickets can be purchased at the BSides Greenville event page.

Dates

March 18-20, 2020

Location
Lodging

There are a variety of lodging accommodations in close proximity to the training location. Feel free to choose your preference.

Food

Meals will not be provided, but there is a variety of food options in the immediate vicinity of the training facility and nearby accommodations. An hour will be provided for lunch, which should be plenty of time to grab a bite at one of the many nearby restaurants.

Travel

The closest airport is Greenville-Spartanburg International Airport (GSP), which is approximately 12 miles away from the training facility and surrounding accommodations.

Other

There is a 12 student minimum that must be met 2 weeks prior to the event or the event may be cancelled, so don't wait to sign up! In the event of a cancellation, paid participants will have the option of a full refund or credit toward a future training opportunity. Credits apply to training organized by Practical Security Services LLC only.

Refunds

All purchases are considered final two weeks from the start date of the event and no refunds will be issued after that time.
