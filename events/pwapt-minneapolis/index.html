Description

This event is a 3-day presentation of Practical Web Application Penetration Testing (PWAPT) with Tim "lanmaster53" Tomes in Minneapolis, MN. Our friends at FRSecure have opened their doors to the public for this event. We are grateful for their support. For specific details regarding course materials and prerequisites, see the PWAPT training page.

Dates

June 19-21, 2019

Location
Lodging

There are a variety of lodging accommodations in close proximity to the training location. Feel free to choose your preference.

Food

Meals will not be provided, but there is a variety of food options in the immediate vicinity of the training facility and nearby accommodations. An hour will be provided for lunch, which should be plenty of time to grab a bite at one of the many nearby restaurants.
